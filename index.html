Sample Dungeon Vault

Sample Dungeon I: The Public Vault

A free archive of curated sound FX, loops, atmospheres, and one-shots — built for indie devs, web audio experiments, and lo-fi rituals. Drag-and-drop ready, game-engine friendly (Godot, Unity, Web Audio).

Includes: • Footsteps, doors, impacts, drones • Glitch FX, ambient loops, UI blips • WAV / OGG formats, clean and ready to drop in

Ideal for: Game jams, browser games, prototypes, dev testing, and low-budget builds.

Sample Dungeon II: The Deep Vault

Advanced layers of sonic corruption, rare, warped, and system-reactive samples built for signal-based audio engines, horror atmospheres, and nonlinear gameplay.

Includes: • Modular sound chains & multi-stems • Reactive ambiences, granular glitches, psychoacoustic FX • Ready for layering, randomization, and custom logic triggers

Ideal for: Immersive game experiences, interactive installations, and audio-heavy design systems.
